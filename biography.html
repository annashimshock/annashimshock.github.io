HE WAS LEFT ON THE DOORSTEP OF THE GOBLIN KING WHO RAISED HIM AS HIS HOME. HE DESPISED HIS PARENTS FOR ABANDONING HIM AND PROMISED HE WOULD GET HIS REVENGE. AFTER MURDERING HIS PARENTS HE WALKED INTO A RANDOM STUDIO WHERE HE SAW SOME PEOPLE VOICE ACTING, SAID, AND I QUOTE, "HOLD MY BEER", AND RECORDED LINES FOR LUIGI. CHARLES MARTINET, SEEING ALL THIS GO DOWN, GAVE A SOFT CHUCKLE AND SAID WITH A SMILE, "CLASSIC BARDAKOFF."
